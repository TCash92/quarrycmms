# Baseline Weekly Summary Template

> **Purpose**: Aggregate all baseline data into a single weekly summary. Supervisors complete this form at the end of each baseline week by consolidating all Downtime Tracking Forms and Work Order Logs.

---

## Instructions

1. **Collect all forms** from technicians at end of week
2. **Verify data** - ensure times are complete and calculations correct
3. **Total all values** into this summary
4. **Store safely** - these numbers determine ROI calculation
5. **Submit to Site Manager** by Monday of following week

---

## Calculation Helpers

### Average Completion Time (B4)

```
Total hours for all completed WOs
─────────────────────────────────  =  Average hours
Number of completed WOs
```

**Example**:
- WO #1: 2 hours
- WO #2: 8 hours
- WO #3: 4 hours
- WO #4: 6 hours

Total: 20 hours / 4 WOs = **5 hours average**

### Total Downtime (A2)

Add up all durations from all Downtime Tracking Forms:

| Form | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Crusher | 3h 30m |
| Conveyor | 1h 15m |
| Loader | 2h 45m |
| **Total** | **7h 30m** |
